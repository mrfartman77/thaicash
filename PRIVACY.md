# ThaiCash Privacy Policy

**Effective: June 2026**

ThaiCash is built to collect nothing.

## What we collect

Nothing. ThaiCash has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising,
no tracking SDKs, and no backend of its own. We do not collect, store, sell,
or share any personal information — because the app never has any.

## What stays on your device

Your settings (home currency, the fee numbers you enter in Setup) and cached
rate data are stored only on your device, and are deleted when you delete the
app.

## Network requests the app makes

ThaiCash fetches publicly available reference data over HTTPS:

- **Mid-market exchange rates** from public rate APIs (open.er-api.com and
  frankfurter.dev).
- **Method catalog, exchange-booth board rates, and venue prices** as static
  JSON files hosted on GitHub.

These requests contain no personal data and no identifiers beyond what any
HTTP request technically includes (such as an IP address, which those
services may see in their ordinary server operations under their own
policies). We receive nothing.

## Location

ThaiCash never requests your location. "Find a booth" and "Find an ATM" rows
open Apple Maps, which handles any location features under Apple's own
privacy policy and permissions.

## Links to providers

"Get started" links open providers' official websites in your browser. Their
privacy policies apply on their sites.

## Children

ThaiCash does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including
children — it collects nothing from everyone.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the update will be published at this URL with a
new effective date.
